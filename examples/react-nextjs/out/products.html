Sign. Secure transaction signing when you connect a wallet with an app.

Interact with all of web3. We facilitate a secure connection between wallets and hundreds of apps, bridging you to DeFi, NFTs, and so much more.
Sign off on your activity. You’ll be prompted to verify each transaction in a seamless, one-click experience.
Step into the multi-chain future. You can connect to a wallet with one or more chains simultaneously and send transactions to different chains at the same time. No more chain switching!

Auth. One-click wallet login to instantly access apps.

Connect with minimal clicks. All you need to do is scan a QR code or click on a deep link to connect with an app. WalletConnect gets to work, establishing the remote pairing in the background.

Chat. Direct messaging between users, using their wallet address.

Coming Soon
Web3’s first wallet-to-wallet messaging feature. You can now chat with your frens, fellow DAO members, and more using just your wallet! All you need is a wallet address, a QR code, or an ENS domain to get the conversation started.
Chat using any wallet and on any chain. Our Chat API works across platforms and chains, facilitating messages between any two WalletConnect-supported wallets.

Push. Notifications of blockchain activity, straight to your wallet.
